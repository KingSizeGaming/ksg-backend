Uploading Your Work

The "Upload File" tab is your go-to for submitting your work. Here’s how you can use it:

  • Select the Game: Start by choosing the game to which your work belongs. This helps in organizing and categorizing your submissions accurately.
  • Upload a Single File: If you have a single file to upload, use the "Single File Upload" option. Click on the button, select your file from your device, and hit upload. It's that simple!
  • Upload a Folder: For multiple files organized in a folder, use the "Upload Folder" option. Select the entire folder from your device and upload it in one go. This keeps all related files together and saves time.

Tracking Your Submissions

The "Dashboard" tab is where you can keep track of all the work you have submitted. Here’s what you can do:

  • View Submissions: You can see a list of all your submitted work by clicking Preview Asset, making it easy to review what has been uploaded.
  • Leave Comments: For any submitted work, you can leave comments. This is useful for providing additional information, clarifications, or any feedback related to the submission.

Managing Your Files

The "File Explorer" tab allows you to download files you have previously uploaded. Here’s how to use it:

  • Browse Files: Navigate through your uploaded files using the File Explorer. This tool is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.
  • Download Files: Simply click the icon next to any file you wish to download. This will save a copy to your device for offline access or further editing.
